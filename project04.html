POP TikR

POP TiKR is an innovative application that seeks to close the distance between customers and small local businesses. It offers a new way to find entertainment, shops, restaurants, and more while saving money and contributing to the local community.

About the Project

For this project, I collaborated closely with both the client and the development team to ensure that the design aligned perfectly with the user's needs and goals. Additionally, I was responsible for creating various graphics, including flyers, stickers, magazine covers, and promotional videos, which were used during the launching event that took place in October 2021 and the #Mylocal campaign."

UI Design

New features were created using Adobe XD and Figma softwares.

Promotional Video

The video was created using Adobe After Effects, Adobe Premiere Pro, and Adobe Audition. The first video was intended to advertise the POP TikR new campaign, and I utilized design principles and storytelling techniques to ensure that the video effectively conveyed the message to new users and encouraged them to download the app. I created the second video to participate in the RE/ACTION showcase competition organized by Algonquin College.
